# Shokuiku Saga RPGCore Concept - Complete Framework

## üìñ Overview

The **Shokuiku Saga RPG** is an anime-inspired food education game that combines classic RPG mechanics with nutrition learning. Players embark on an epic journey through the Food Kingdom, defeat manifestations of unhealthy eating habits, and become the ultimate Food Guardian.

---

## üåç Setting: The Food Kingdom

A vibrant, anime-inspired world divided into five major regions, each representing a food group:

### Regions
1. **Grain Plains** - Carbohydrates and whole grains
2. **Veggie Forest** - Vegetables, vitamins, minerals, and fiber
3. **Protein Peaks** - Both plant and animal proteins
4. **Dairy Valley** - Calcium, probiotics, and dairy alternatives
5. **Fruit Isles** - Fruits, natural sugars, and hydration

### Core Goal
Restore balance to the Food Kingdom by learning about nutrition, cooking, and mindful eating. Combat the invasion of "Diet Myths" that confuse teens about healthy eating.

---

## üéÆ Game Mechanics

### 1. **Classic RPG Progression**
- **EatXP System**: Earn points from quests, battles, and mini-games
- **Leveling**: Progress through levels to unlock new regions and story chapters
- **Skill Tree**: Unlock cooking techniques, mindfulness practices, and food knowledge
- **Inventory**: Collect ingredients and food items for quests and battles

### 2. **Food-Based Battle System**
Turn-based combat where all moves are food-related:
- **Veggie Shield** - Defense boost through vegetable consumption
- **Protein Punch** - Attack power from protein sources
- **Hydration Heal** - Restore stamina with water and healthy fluids
- **Grain Ground** - Create stability with whole grain energy
- **Fruit Force** - Elemental attacks using various fruit powers

### 3. **Educational Challenges**
- **Nutrition Quizzes** - Quick challenges disguised as puzzles
- **Cooking Mini-Games** - Hands-on recipe preparation
- **Ingredient Collection** - Find and gather foods from different regions
- **Balanced Meal Challenges** - Arrange ingredients for nutritious meals

### 4. **Mini-Games Collection**
- **Bento Box Crafting** - Arrange ingredients for balanced meals
- **Smoothie Mixing** - Combine fruits and vegetables for health boosts
- **Spot the Balanced Meal** - Identify nutritious meal compositions
- **Ingredient Memory** - Match foods to their health benefits
- **Cooking Simulation** - Follow recipe steps correctly

## üìö Complete Storyline Arc

### **Prologue: The Broken Plate / Diet Myths Invasion**
- **Narrative**: The Food Kingdom's sacred plate shatters, and Diet Myths invade, spreading confusion about nutrition among teens
- **Objective**: Chosen as the "Food Guardian" to restore harmony
- **Teaching Focus**: Introduction to balanced nutrition

### **Chapter 1: Grain Plains**
- **Narrative**: Learn about carbohydrates and whole grains
- **Quest Theme**: "Energy for Exams" - carbs & balanced meals
- **Boss**: **Refined Carb Phantom** - Drains energy by promoting refined carbs
- **Reward**: Carbohydrate Knowledge Badge, +1.2x EatXP multiplier

### **Chapter 2: Veggie Forest**
- **Narrative**: Discover vitamins, minerals, and fiber
- **Quest Theme**: "Glow-Up Quest" - fruits & veggies for skin/strength
- **Boss**: **Junk Goblin** - Tempts villagers with fried snacks
- **Reward**: Vegetable Knowledge Badge, "Rainbow Salad" recipe

### **Chapter 3: Protein Peaks**
- **Narrative**: Balance plant vs. animal proteins
- **Quest Theme**: "Sports Power-Up" - proteins & hydration
- **Boss**: **Overload Ogre** - Represents excess consumption
- **Reward**: Protein Knowledge Badge, +25% Strength Boost

### **Chapter 4a: Dairy Valley**
- **Narrative**: Explore calcium, probiotics, and dairy alternatives
- **Boss**: **Lactose Leviathan** - Represents lactose intolerance myths
- **Reward**: Dairy Knowledge Badge

### **Chapter 4b: Fruit Isles**
- **Narrative**: Discover natural sugars and fruit vitamins
- **Boss**: **Sugar Siren** - Tempts with energy drinks and excess sugar
- **Reward**: Fruit Knowledge Badge

### **Chapter 5: The Balanced Feast (Final Chapter)**
- **Narrative**: Reforge the sacred plate by combining all food groups
- **Quest Theme**: "Late-Night Boss Battle" - healthy snacking vs. fast food temptations
- **Boss**: **Chaos Diet Dragon** - Symbolizes fad diets, body image pressures, and misinformation
- **Reward**: Sacred Plate Restored, Food Guardian Title, Ultimate Recipe Collection

## üîÑ Implementation Notes

### Core Principles
1. **Education First**: All gameplay should reinforce nutrition learning
2. **Character Agency**: Hero's anime character choice influences personal storyline
3. **Accessibility**: Difficulty options and skip mechanics for all ages
4. **Community**: Social features encourage sharing and collaboration
5. **Real-World Impact**: Game motivates actual dietary improvements

### Design Considerations
- Mini-games should take 5-10 minutes to complete
- Quests should have clear, achievable objectives
- Boss battles should teach mechanics through gameplay
- Dialogue should balance instruction with entertainment
- Pacing: Early chapters introduce mechanics, later chapters increase complexity

### Content Updates
- Seasonal events tied to real food seasons/holidays
- Limited-time collaborations with anime properties
- Community challenges with rotating themes
- Regular balance updates based on player feedback

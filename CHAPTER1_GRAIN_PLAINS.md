## ğŸ® Educational Focus

### Core Learning Objectives
1. **Carbohydrates as Energy Source**
   - Carbs are the PRIMARY fuel for your brain and body
   - All carbs break down to glucose for energy
   - Understanding carb types matters for sustained performance

2. **Whole Grains vs Refined Carbs**
   - Whole grains = COMPLETE package (bran, germ, endosperm)
   - Refined carbs = Stripped of nutrients (mostly starch)
   - Whole grains provide FIBER, VITAMINS, MINERALS
   - Refined carbs cause energy CRASHES

3. **Fiber Benefits**
   - Makes you feel full LONGER
   - Aids digestive health
   - Feeds beneficial gut bacteria
   - Provides sustained energy (not crashes)

4. **Real-World Application**
   - Brown rice vs white rice
   - Whole wheat vs white bread
   - Steel-cut oats vs instant oats
   - Reading nutrition labels

## ğŸ§  Educational Design Philosophy

### Engagement Through Story
- Farmers in crisis create urgency and stakes
- Phantom's dialogue reveals its biases
- Victory feels earned through knowledge application
- Personal connection to NPCs (villagers) increases care

### Learning Through Gameplay
- **Quiz:** Direct nutrition knowledge
- **Cooking:** Practical meal selection
- **Collection:** Grain identification
- **Mini-game:** Speed + knowledge combo
- **Boss Battle:** Apply all learnings through myth-countering

### Myth-Counter System
Each myth is:
1. **Believable:** Addresses common misconceptions teens have
2. **Refutable:** Clear nutrition facts counter the myth
3. **Actionable:** Knowledge translates to in-game advantage
4. **Educational:** Players learn real nutrition science

### Teen Appeal
- Action-oriented boss battle (not turn-based strategy)
- Anime-style villain dialogue (dramatic and fun)
- Collectible companion (Quinoa Spirit)
- Achievement feeling from defeating boss
- Visual rewards (outfit cosmetics)
